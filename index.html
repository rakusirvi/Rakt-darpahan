How Rakt Darpan Works

Our platform connects hospitals, blood banks, and patients to ensure timely availability of blood when needed most.

Hospital Registration

Hospitals can register and update their blood stock in real-time for patients to access.

Easy Search

Patients and donors can search for blood availability by location and blood type.

Real-time Updates

Get instant notifications about blood availability and urgent requirements.

Secure Database

All data is securely stored and accessible only to authorized personnel.
